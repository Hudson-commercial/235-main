ABOUT



This 3 story office building is adjacent to the Mid-Hudson Civic Center and located at the hub of City of Poughkeepsie activity. Transportation connections are excellent. It is near the Mid-Hudson Bridge, the Metro-North train station, the East-West Arterial (Rt. 44/55) and on the local bus routes. Easy access to Route 9. The Dutchess County and City of Poughkeepsie Courts and the Dutchess County Records Building with DMV are located within the two-block area along with the historic Bardavon Opera House. It is adjacent to a parking garage and there are several parking lots nearby. Within several blocks there are a variety of restaurant both upscale and casual and one of the best Italian delicatessens in the area.
